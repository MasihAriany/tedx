LOGO CONTEST

Submit your designed Logo for our Logo Contest
Win a FREE SEAT in TEDxShirazUniversity!



  • Requirments


    please Read these rules carefully.
    If any of the Requirments are not satisfied, your work won't be selected.

فارسی Download PDF

Your Logo must contain the main title (TEDxShirazUniversity).
Once you signed up for the contest, it will be sent to you.

You'll need to use a Software which works with Vectors and not Bitmap.
So use Softwares like Corel and not the ones like photoshop.

Your output Resolution must be exactly 300dpi .
Please avoid sending less or more Resolutions.


You are required to use ONLY RED, BLACK and WHITE for your Logo's Color.
For White the specification is :
(Hex: #FFFFFF - RGB: 255, 255, 255 - CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0 - HSV: 0, 0, 100)
And for black and Red as depicted here.
Please Note that no other RED or BLACK color will be accepted.


The whole Logo must be fit into a Unit ( a Hypothetical shape ). Think of a Circle or Rectangle for instance.

The subject of your design must represent our Theme or ShirazUniversity.
visit this link to see some WorldWide samples.

The Output format must be PNG. Please avoid sending JPG or any other format.

Note that the design must be your own original, unpublished work.
we will need to have your project files and layers if your work got selected.
any duplicated design will be removed from the contest.
